Game Guide

How to Play

Monster Survivors is an action-packed roguelike survival game where your goal is simple: stay alive as long as possible while defeating hordes of monsters.

Controls

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Your character attacks automatically when enemies are in range
  • Collect experience gems to level up
  • Choose new abilities or power-ups when you level up

Tips for Survival

  1. Keep moving to avoid being surrounded
  2. Focus on creating synergies between your abilities
  3. Prioritize survival over chasing experience gems
  4. Don't hesitate to use powerful area-of-effect abilities when surrounded
